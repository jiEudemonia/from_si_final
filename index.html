Transistor

After fabrication, Si becomes wafer as shown on left with billions of transistors siting on it.

A transistor is a semiconductor device used to amplify or switch electronic signals andelectrical power. It can translate analog signal to digital signal, which is "0" or "1".

Logic Gate

Transistors cooperate with each other forming Logic Gates.

A logic gate is an elementary building block of a digital circuit. Most logic gates have two inputs and one output. At any given moment, every terminal is in one of the two binary conditions low (0) or high (1), represented by different voltage levels.

ALU

Logic Gates with logic functions like "and", "or", and "nor" can finally make ALUs, which is Arithmetic Logic Unit, a digital electronic circuit that performs arithmetic and bitwise logical operations on integer binary numbers.

Processor

Many different functional ALUs along with other components constitute a processor.

A central processing unit (CPU) is the electronic circuitry within a computer that carries out the instructions of a computer program by performing the basic arithmetic, logical, control and input/output operations specified by the instructions.
